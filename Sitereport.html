Introduction


The report (250 word minimum) will contain your experience of learning the basics of web development, coding, design and debugging process. This should be a log or an informal conversation explaining your development experience. Include your ups and downs!A ‘reflective discussion of your module experience’. You will set out in the report the development of the site over the term and focus on both the technical and design of sites user interface (UI). Explain the design decisions you made, how you chose the font/colours and link to any sites which influenced your website’s design. Format the report with relevant sections, dates and headings. You should include images of your progress and document any changes you made over time.On this page, include screenshots of validation reports showing that your pages HTML and CSS validate.
