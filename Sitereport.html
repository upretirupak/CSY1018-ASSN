Report


Introduction


A portfolio website is a unique way to showcase our work and let others know about ourself. It’s like an evergreen platform for our projects, case studies, and information about us. In addition, it’s one of the best ways to express our personality, experience, and capabilities.Having our own website means customers are always able to find us and if interested, reach us. If we don’t have an online presence nowadays, we are behind the times. A portfolio is a great way for photographers, designers, developers and a wide range of artists to present their work online. It lets us reflect our identity through our works – photos, graphic design, sketches, etc (Linda ,Ragaine 2021).

Aims and Objectives


This was the webpage developed with the aim to create a personal portfolio.All over a portfolio website is created to showcase our work whether we are a individual or a company.It cope up to stand out from the crowd, show our uniqueness, build trust, and make sure that we are available.Creating a personal portfolio is a plus point for:
1.Web design
2.Web development (outsourcing, online shop)
3.Graphic design (logo design, corporate ID, flyers, catalogs, packaging)
4.Marketing (digital marketing, print design, SEO, PPC advertising)
5.Art design (illustration, video, audio, photography)
So to go with the digital trend and to publish out it has been a must.
